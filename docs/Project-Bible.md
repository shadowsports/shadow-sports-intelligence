# Shadow Sports Intelligence
## Project Bible

**Version:** 0.1
**Status:** Foundation Phase

---

# 1. Mission Statement

Shadow Sports Intelligence is a sports analytics platform built around one core principle:

**Verified Data. Smarter Decisions.**

Shadow is designed to provide users with trusted sports information by validating data before presenting analysis.

The platform will focus initially on Major League Baseball (MLB) and later expand into additional sports.

---

# 2. Product Vision

Shadow Sports Intelligence is not a prediction website.

The primary goal is to provide:

- Verified sports information
- Advanced analytics
- Data confidence ratings
- Player and team insights
- Transparent analysis

Users should always understand:
- What information is confirmed
- What information is estimated
- What information is unavailable

---

# 3. Initial Sport Focus

## Major League Baseball (MLB)

Version 1 will focus on:

- MLB schedules
- Starting pitchers
- Lineups
- Injuries
- Player statistics
- Team statistics
- Bullpen information
- Game environment factors

---

# 4. Core Feature: Shadow Intelligence Score

Every game will receive a Data Intelligence Score from 0-100.

This score measures the reliability and completeness of available information.

## Score Categories

| Category | Weight |
|---|---:|
| Schedule Verification | 10 |
| Starting Pitcher Verification | 25 |
| Lineup Verification | 25 |
| Injury Information | 15 |
| Bullpen Availability | 15 |
| Weather Information | 5 |
| Data Freshness | 5 |

Total: 100

---

# 5. Data Verification Engine

Shadow will compare information from multiple sources.

The system will identify:

- Confirmed information
- Conflicting information
- Missing information
- Outdated information

Shadow will never present uncertain information as confirmed.
